Llangollen Canoe Club

We may be biased but we think we are based in the best place for paddlesports we could be!!
With summer evening sessions on the river Dee at TNR Outdoors (Mile End Mill) and winter pool sessions that include a wave machine at Plas Madoc, it doesn't get much better.
Add to that, club members who are always out taking part in a wide range of paddlesport and it seems, if this is what you are interested in, you have come to the right place!!

What We Do

We are a canoe and kayak club based in beautiful Llangollen in North Wales!

With the river Dee on our doorstep and easy access to all North Wales has to offer, there is something for everyone. Whether you want a chilled out day on the canal or a local lake, to run the river with your friends (old and new), or get to a feature and throw some tricks this is for you.

Our main club sessions are on a monday night and as well as independent peer paddles, we have a range of coached and led sessions for all skill levels. Junior sessions are also available so the whole family can paddle together!

No matter how much experience you have, just turn up with a smile and we can pretty much guarantee it will be bigger by the time you leave!
